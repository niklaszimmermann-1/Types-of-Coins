Different Types of Coins

About Coins

Coins have been used for centuries as a means of trade, culture, and history. They carry stories of empires, traditions, and artistry, making them valuable not just in money but in heritage.

Types of Coins

  • Ancient Coins – From Greek drachmas to Roman denarii, they reflect the history of great civilizations.
  • Commemorative Coins – Issued to honor events, leaders, or anniversaries, often with intricate designs.
  • Rare World Coins – Scarce pieces from around the globe, prized by collectors for their uniqueness.
